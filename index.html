The Ant simulation has 5 rules!

  1. Harvesters are orange. They are random walkers with a bias towards blue pheremones. Searchers are yellow, and are smooth random walkers made with Gaussian Noise
  2. When Searchers find food they drop a strong pheremone. When Harvesters find food they drop a weak pheremone
  3. Running the hill takes food. Producing ants also takes food. They only way to gain food is for harvesters to bring them back
  4. Any ant has a chance to die when not resting in the hive
  5. Searchers spawn every 10 seconds. Harvesters spawn anytime an ant comes back to the hill
